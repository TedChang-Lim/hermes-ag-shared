## 2. Prologue — A Word from the Author

We live in an era where an individual's productivity is historically at its most powerful.

As an engineer, I have spent a lifetime analyzing the structure of technology. As a photographer and video producer, I have created visual beauty. As an AI researcher, I have ceaselessly explored how artificial intelligence can transform human life and creation. While performing all these roles simultaneously, the wall I repeatedly hit was just one: the 'limitation of time and resources.'

When large language models (LLMs) first emerged, I ran AI assistants daily to help with work and research. The performance was excellent, but the hundreds of dollars in API costs and subscription fees charged each month were a major barrier for individual creators and small cooperatives.

"Can I create an AI that works just for me — cheaper, safely, even without an internet connection?"

The answer to this question is contained in this book.
Thanks to the API price revolution triggered by DeepSeek, cloud API usage fees have effectively approached 'near zero,' and with the remarkable advancement of Apple Silicon MacBooks, the era of running server-grade AI models directly on your desk has arrived.

I have built and collaborate 24/7 with my two artificial intelligence agent partners: **'Haena'** and **'AG (Advantage Guide).'** When Haena writes brilliant plans and manuscript drafts, AG silently and meticulously verifies and refines technical errors. This book itself is the result of a perfect team effort between myself and the two AI agents.

This book is written for everyone — from general creators without a technical background to engineers and researchers looking to build a maximally efficient local AI research environment. From ultra-cost-effective cloud setups at the price of a cup of coffee to uncensored offline local AI built directly inside your MacBook, it provides an immediately actionable roadmap.

The technology is already ready. Now it's your turn to become the commander of a one-person enterprise with just one MacBook.
