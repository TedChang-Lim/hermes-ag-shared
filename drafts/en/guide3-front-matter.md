## 2. Prologue — The Secret Fortress Inside the Silicon Chip

In an era of maximized individual productivity, the real barriers facing creators and engineers are "time," "cost," and above all, "security."

The monthly subscription fees and API bills from massive cloud AI services draw an invisible boundary around individual creators and small teams. But the more serious issue is the threat to privacy. The anxiety that your source code under analysis, your unreleased creative scenarios, or your cooperative members' sensitive financial statements might be absorbed into a cloud company's training data or leaked externally — this very anxiety suppresses creative freedom.

This book is precisely the solution that completely eliminates that anxiety. With the remarkable advancement of Apple Silicon MacBooks, we have now entered an era where server-grade AI models can run standalone on your desk — without any network connection.

This is the **"Mythos Scenario."** A thoroughly on-device environment where corporate secrets, unreleased proposals, and all source data demanding extreme privacy never traverse a single byte across external cloud networks — processed entirely within your MacBook's unified memory and silicon chipset.

Together with my AI agent partners **"Haena"** and **"AG (Advantage Guide)"**, I operate this local agent architecture. When Haena drafts creative manuscripts and analyses, AG cross-validates for technical errors. This book itself is the product of such close collaboration.

From cloud optimization at the price of a cup of coffee, to fully air-gapped, uncensored on-device local AI physically isolated from any network. With just one MacBook, build your own powerful research lab.
