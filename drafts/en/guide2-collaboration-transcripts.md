#### 1.1 Why Are AI API Prices a Problem?

ChatGPT Plus is $20/month, Claude Pro is $20/month, Gemini Pro is $20/month...

A solo entrepreneur who wants to properly use an AI assistant needs at least 2~3 subscriptions. $60~100/month. That's $720~1,200/year.

**That's not the only problem.** Using the API directly (for developers) is even more expensive.

| Service | Input Price (1M tokens) | Output Price (1M tokens) |
|--------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| GPT-4o | $5.00 | $15.00 |
| Claude 3.5 Sonnet | $3.00 | $15.00 |
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | $1.25 | $5.00 |

Even just 10 million tokens per day costs **$58.75 per day based on GPT-4o**. That easily exceeds $1,700 per month.

**This is the biggest reason people "can't use an AI assistant."**

---

#### 1.2 DeepSeek V4, Sparking a Price Revolution

In December 2025, China's DeepSeek announced the V4 series and shook the market. Not simply because the performance is good. It's because **the price is at 1/10 the level of competitors**.

And on May 31, 2026, the decision DeepSeek made:
> **Permanently discounted V4 Pro and Flash API prices by 75%.**

| Model | Input (1M tokens) | Output (1M tokens) |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| DeepSeek V4 Pro | **$0.435** | **$0.87** |
| DeepSeek V4 Flash | **$0.14** | **$0.28** |

Compared to GPT-4o, **Pro is 1/12, Flash is 1/35** the cost.

---

#### 1.3 Context Caching: The Secret to Using It All Day for the Price of Coffee

On top of this, DeepSeek supports **Context Caching**. The same system prompts or frequently referenced documents are stored on the server, and when reused, are calculated at up to **90% discounted** prices.

| Category | No Caching | Caching Applied (85% hit) | Actual Daily Cost |
|----------|-----------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| V4 Pro | $0.87 | $0.13 | **$0.13** |
| V4 Flash | $0.28 | $0.057 | **$0.057** |

Even processing 10 million tokens per day: **GPT-4o is $58.75, DeepSeek V4 Flash is $0.057**.

The difference is an incredible **1,030x**.

---

#### 1.4 Mimo 2.5: For Image Analysis

When you need image analysis or multimodal work, use Xiaomi's **Mimo 2.5** from China.

| Category | Price |

| Mimo 2.5 Input | $0.14 / 1M tokens |
