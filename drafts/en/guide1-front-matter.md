## 2. Prologue — A Word from the Author

Technological progress always wears two faces. On one hand, it makes our lives dramatically more convenient; on the other, it erects barriers to entry through the enormous cost of owning and maintaining that technology.

I have worked my entire life as an engineer on the IT frontlines. Today, I live as a photographer and video producer capturing the world through a camera and direction, while simultaneously forging a path as an AI researcher. As both a creator and researcher who must analyze vast amounts of data, write, code, and produce content every day, an artificial intelligence (AI) assistant was nothing short of a revolutionary savior.

However, as I subscribed to multiple well-known AI services and increased my API call volume, the monthly bills reached several hundred dollars. For a solo creator or small cooperative, those recurring monthly subscription fees carried a weight that could never be ignored.

"Is it possible to keep intelligence at the highest level while dramatically cutting costs?"

This book was born from that very question.
And in May 2026, when DeepSeek declared a permanent 75% discount on API pricing, the answer finally arrived.

This book is a practical guide for setting up and leveraging your own powerful AI agents (Cline, Hermes, etc.) on your own computer — at an ultra-low cost of around $4/month — even without complex IT expertise or expensive paid subscription services.

To all creators, solo entrepreneurs, and researcher colleagues who have hesitated to embrace the power of AI collaboration due to cost barriers, I hope this book becomes a sturdy pair of wings.

**Ted Chang (임창식)**
